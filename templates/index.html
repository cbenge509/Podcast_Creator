Frequently Asked Questions

What types of PDFs work best? +
The kind with words... lots of words (in English), and preferably about something you're interested in. The more words, the better the podcast! I have tested it with PDFs that include just images and it doesn't do a good job of making sense out of those right now.
How long does it take to generate a podcast? +
It depends. If you're not converting to another language and you're sticking with 300 words, it should take under a minute. The more words you want, the longer it will take. If you're converting to another language, it will take even longer.
What audio formats are supported? +
Right now, this only generates MP3 files. I can add more formats if there's a demand for it.
